ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Donte. I am currently a graduate student at University of California, Berkeley. I was born and raised in the East Bay, graduating from College Park High School in Pleasant Hill, CA. After graduating high school, I went on to served in the Air Force for 12 years as an Avionics Technician and a Human Resources Director with assignments in Japan, Southwest Asia, Texas, Mississippi and San Diego. During my last 4 years in the military I completed my Bachelor's degree in Business Administration with a focus on Human Resource Management. My most recent job was as a Section Chief for Military Personnel Actions for the Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps based at San Diego State University.
